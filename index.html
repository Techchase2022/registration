ROUND 1: Eternity Battle

ROUND 2: Ball Maze

In this round the participant has to blow up a ball by his mouth, hold it there and  throw it in the basket by crossing the maze without any physical contact to the ball.

ROUND 3: Dangal (Wild Card entry)

ROUND 4: Dark Hunt (Night event)

Pillow fight is a brand new event in the series of non-tech events in projections. This game requires both physical as well as mental balance. There will be pillows of round shape that will be provided to each player. The players have to balance themselves as well as try to make their opponent fall off ! Pillow to sleep or pillow to protect! Yeah, Pillow will be your saviour to rescue you.

ROUND 1:

In this round, every participant has to fight with an opponent, which will be decided by selecting chits. For example: If there are 48 participants they will be divided into 24-24. They will select any one chit from the bunch of 24 chits. Participants with the same numbers will fight against each other. As it is an elimination round there will be only one chance.

ROUND 2:

Now there are only 24 participants left. They will be divided in six groups consisting of 4 members. The fight will be held between 1st and 6th group, 2nd and 5th group, 3rd and  4th group. The winning groups will be sent to the next round.

ROUND 3:

After round 2 only 12 participants will be left. They will fight against each other while balancing themselves in an upright position on the boxes and they also have to eliminate their opponents in 2 minutes.

ROUND 4:

Top 6 participants will make it to the final showdown wherein, they will fight with each other. But now it’s not only to eliminate the opponent, but they also have to race with time. The winners will be decided on the basis of time they have taken to eliminate their respective opponent.

If the participant uses any other equipment, then he/she will be disqualified.

While fighting, if your legs touch the ground you will be eliminated.

In round 2 if there is a tie between teams then there will be a super match between the two. The team members have to choose the member who will represent the team to fight with the opponent.

In round three if there is a tie, it means they have failed to knock down the opponent in two minutes. In this case, the winner will be decided by a surprise round.

The game doesn’t include any material which may cause any harm.

It is a game which is a combination of the quiz, treasure hunt, and marketing. In this game, the participants have to first face the quiz round followed by the treasure hunt and the marketing round. The top 5 teams of round 1 will get extra points in the form of clues for the next treasure hunt round. Rest of the teams have to start the treasure hunt from the scratch while the winners of the previous round would continue with the extra clues which they have earned in round 1. In treasure hunt they have to solve the riddles to collect the items and after collecting them they’ll enter the marketing round. Here they have to make usable things from the things they have found. After creating their item they have to try to convince the co-ordinator to buy it from them. A challenging event to check your level of brainstorming !!

ROUND 1: (Bonus Round)

The participants  will be taking the quiz round in order to earn the bonus clues for the next round(15 questions at max.)The completion of  round 1 will leave us with top 5 teams who will get the bonus clues on the basis of their finishing ranks. No team will get eliminated. The teams not finishing in the top 5 would be starting the round 2 treasure hunt from the scratch while on the other hand top 5 teams would be playing with the extra clues.

ROUND 2: (Treasure Hunt)

Here the teams have to potray their logical skills as they have to solve the clues to get their respective items which are to be used in the next marketing round. This round will lead to the elimination of teams. We will be left with 5 teams to advance to the next round.

ROUND 3: (Marketing Round)

The top 5 teams now have to work with their imagination skills and have to make a product from the things they found in round 2 After creating a product they have to sell it to the co-ordinators. The team which is able to convince the co-ordinators and sell at the best price will be declared as the winners.

The participants are not supposed to take any sort of help from the volunteers
